User Guide

Work with your digital employees in minutes.

DoWhiz delivers a team of digital employees you can message directly. Today, the simplest way to start is email. Soon, you will be able to reach the team by other channels as well.

Last updated: February 14, 2026

2. Write a clear request

Great requests lead to great outcomes. Include:

  • Your goal and preferred format (bullet list, doc, spreadsheet, code, and so on).
  • Important context, constraints, and background links.
  • Deadlines or time sensitivity.
  • Any examples or style references you want matched.

3. Share files and sources

Attach files, data, or links directly in your message. DoWhiz will use them to complete the work and send results back to you in the same thread.

4. What happens next

  • Your message is triaged and routed to the right tools.
  • Work is executed with the necessary context and data.
  • Results are returned to your inbox with clear next steps.

5. Scheduling and follow-ups

If you need recurring work, describe the cadence and expectations (for example, weekly reports every Monday). DoWhiz will confirm scheduling details before proceeding.

6. Support and feedback

If something is unclear or incomplete, reply in the same thread. For general questions or support, email admin@dowhiz.com.
