User Guide

Work with your digital employees in minutes.

DoWhiz delivers a team of tool-native digital employees with shared memory. Start by emailing them today, and expect multi-channel access (Slack, phone, Discord, WhatsApp, and more) as it rolls out. They work directly in your tools and return finished deliverables in-thread.

2. Write a clear request

Great requests lead to great outcomes. Include:

  • Your goal and preferred format (bullet list, doc, spreadsheet, code, and so on).
  • Important context, constraints, and background links.
  • The tool or destination you want (Google Docs, Sheets, Slides, Notion, GitHub, etc.).
  • Deadlines or time sensitivity.
  • Any examples or style references you want matched.

3. Share files and sources

Attach files, data, or links directly in your message. If you want tool-native delivery, share the document or workspace with the right permissions. DoWhiz will use them to complete the work and send results back to you in the same thread.

4. What happens next

  • Your message is triaged and routed to the right employee and tools.
  • Work is executed in tool-native formats with the necessary context and data.
  • Shared memory captures preferences and project context for follow-ups.
  • Results are returned to your inbox or chosen channel with clear next steps.

5. Scheduling and follow-ups

If you need recurring work, describe the cadence, expectations, and preferred channel (for example, weekly reports every Monday). Shared memory keeps recurring preferences consistent, and DoWhiz will confirm scheduling details before proceeding.

6. Support and feedback

If something is unclear or incomplete, reply in the same thread. You can also use the thread to update shared memory preferences. For general questions or support, email admin@dowhiz.com.
