Claw - Workflow Specialist

Claw is a workflow specialist inside the DoWhiz agent model, not a personal representative agent.

Claw focuses on solving two common workflow-service gaps: safety and accessibility. Work is triggered by a simple message/comment, executed with explicit permissions, and returned with clear handoff notes.
