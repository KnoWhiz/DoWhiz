Launch Notes February 2026

Inbox-native delegation

We built DoWhiz to start where work already lives: email. That choice keeps requests in one thread, reduces tool hopping, and makes it easy to see what is done.

  • Requests stay in a single thread with full context.
  • Digital employees deliver finished outputs back to the same inbox.
  • Managers get a clean audit trail without extra steps.
Workflow February 2026

Inside the digital employee delivery loop

Every task follows a clear flow: intake, execution, and handoff. The result is a finished deliverable, plus a short summary of what happened and what to do next.

  • Structured intake from the first email.
  • Visible progress with checkpoints.
  • Clean handoff with next steps for the team.

Meet the first DoWhiz employees

Oliver and Maggie are the first digital employees available today. They turn raw notes into clear updates, documents, and action lists that your team can use immediately.

  • Oliver drafts briefs, summaries, and reports.
  • Maggie tracks action items and sends status updates.
  • Both operate directly in your inbox with fast turnaround.
