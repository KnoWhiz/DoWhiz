Launching the DoWhiz blog

The DoWhiz blog is where we will publish product notes, new workflow templates, and case studies from teams that use digital employees every day. Expect short, practical updates you can forward to your team.

Have a topic request? Email us at admin@dowhiz.com and we will build a post around it.

Inbox-first workflows that actually ship

The best results happen when a request includes scope, expected output, and timing. We share our go-to templates for handoffs, check-ins, and final delivery so your team can move fast without extra meetings.

Want the templates? Ask Oliver or Maggie and we will send them as a starter pack.

From request to delivery in one thread

DoWhiz keeps work in your existing inbox. We cover what happens after you send a task, how we confirm requirements, and the artifacts you receive back (docs, slides, code, or status updates).

Need a walkthrough for your team? We can host an onboarding session—just email us.
