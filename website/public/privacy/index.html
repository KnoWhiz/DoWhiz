Privacy Policy

Privacy that respects how you work.

DoWhiz is an email-first digital employee service. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have when you use DoWhiz.

Last updated: February 14, 2026

1. Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide and data needed to run the service.

  • Contact details such as name, email address, and company information.
  • Task content, attachments, and any instructions you send to DoWhiz.
  • Usage data such as log files, browser type, device identifiers, and IP address.
  • Data from connected services only when you authorize an integration.

2. How we use information

We use your information to deliver and improve the service.

  • Operate DoWhiz, fulfill tasks, and return results to you.
  • Secure the platform, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Communicate about updates, support, and administrative notices.
  • Improve product quality using aggregate and de-identified insights.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information.

  • Service providers that help us host, secure, and maintain DoWhiz.
  • Third-party services you explicitly connect for task completion.
  • Law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

4. Data retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the service and meet legal requirements. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

5. Security

We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including encryption in transit and at rest. No system can guarantee absolute security.

6. Your choices

  • Access, update, or delete your information by contacting support.
  • Revoke integrations by disconnecting them in your account.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, email us at admin@dowhiz.com.

7. Children's privacy

DoWhiz is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post updates here and notify you if changes are material.
