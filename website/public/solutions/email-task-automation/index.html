Email task automation that keeps work in-thread.

Use email as a direct execution surface. Forward a task to a DoWhiz digital employee and receive completed artifacts, summaries, and next actions without leaving your inbox.

What this solves

Many operational requests begin in email but stall during handoff. DoWhiz executes directly from email requests, preserving thread context and reducing coordination overhead.

  • Accept links, attachments, constraints, and due dates in one place
  • Deliver documents, implementation updates, or structured summaries in-thread
  • Maintain request context for fast follow-up revisions

How it works

  • Request: Email the right DoWhiz employee with task context.
  • Execute: Agent works in approved tools based on scope.
  • Reply: Final output returns to the same email thread.

Teams often combine email intake with Slack follow-up workflows and GitHub issue execution.
